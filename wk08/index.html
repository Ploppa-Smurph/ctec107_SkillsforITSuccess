Creativity

in-born trait combined with a set of skills that can be learned, developed, and utilized in daily problem solving
Creativity is a key soft skill
thinking of new and innovative ways to do tasks and is a characteristic of a High-Performance leader.
use the 5W + 1H method by asking these questions:
  • Who?
  • What?
  • When?
  • Where?
  • Why?
  • How?
Brainstorming is a technique for generating new ideas.
  • Free flowing random process nothing held back
  • All ideas are put on the table and then fine-tuned to arrive at a final solution
  • Brainstorming is useful for attacking specific rather than general issues.

Mind Mapping: diagram used to visually organize the vital info
  • Start at center of blank page
  • Use image or shape for your central idea
  • Use color throughout
  • Connect your branches to the central image or idea
  • Make your branches curved
  • Use one key word per line
  • Use images throughout
Ways to become more creative -- the Six Thinking Hats Method. 
• White hat:  Recognizes that information is power, it is the gathering 
        information and focuses on facts. 

• Blue hat:  Manages the process.  It involves listing the rules and how the 
        problem will be solved in steps.

• Green hat:  Focuses on new ideas, creative concepts and, alternatives.

• Yellow hat:  Helps explore the feasibility and identify value and benefit.

• Red hat:  Signifies feelings and emotions.  It could be the use of intuition 
        and gut feelings.

• Black hat:  Is use of judgment or playing the “devil’s advocate”.  Identifying 
        what can go wrong and assessing potential problems.
