First Impression is a function of self-image
how you perceive yourself largely influences how the world perceives you
Self-Image is how you view yourself, your self-worth and your abilities.

Planned vs Un-planned first impressions --
Planned First Impression - ie: job interview, meeting with clients, romantic occasions
Unplanned First Impression - Walking to the office, When you are traveling, when out shopping or eating

Making a 1st Impression

  • Positive Body Language
  • Bright Smile and Handshake
  • Be Yourself
  • Blend In
  • Punctuality
  • Talk to Convince
  • Positive Mental Attitude

Time Management

art of managing time effectively so that the right time is allocated to the right activity
Time Management is self-management
  • Planning and Prioritize
  • Avoid Procrastination
  • Set Goals and Deadlines
  • Focus on Task Completion

Procrastination

Procrastinate because you are overwhelmed or you are not looking forward to the task.

Q1- Urgent and Important - Immediate - must be done today Q2- Not-Urgent but Important - Can be done tomorrow Q3- Urgent but not important - Do this week Q4- Not Urgent and Not Important - Social Activity
