notes on Kwame Christian's Talk business consultant and negotiation consultant - conflict management. kwame loves cinnamon toast crunch - his wife ate his cereal and so he ate her granola.
conflict can be intimidating and infuriating - society evolved faster than our brains and we have fight or flight reflex. amegdalya highjack - fight, flight, or freeze.
Kwame grew up in Tiffon Ohio -- it was an all white area and he looked and sounded different.

Conflict is an opportunity - you can repair valuable relationships or remove malignant relationships
Compassionate Curiosity we need to listen more effectively. need to be able to realize we can be wrong, or that we might lose a relationship.
Slow down and ask High Level Questions - use your PreFrontal Cortex (center for logical thinking) 'listen i hear you are upset. what are your concerns, how can i help and make this better.'

in your personal life the relationship is closer and the stakes get higher
